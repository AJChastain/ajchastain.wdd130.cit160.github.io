Da Bomb Nom Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide basic recipes with easy-to-follow instructions, complete with photos. The intros are brief so you can get to the real reason you're here: the recipes! No lengthy blogs to slog through; just great food, made by you!

Audience

Da Bomb Nom is for beginning bakers and cooks or anyone who wants to try a recipe they're inexperienced at. This site is for ordinary people who want to prepare something yummy without sifting through verbose food blogs or overcomplicated instructions.
