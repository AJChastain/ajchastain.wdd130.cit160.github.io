Amenities

Free Public Services

We have ample parking available.

Our temple shuttle runs every half hour, so you'll never miss a session.

Easy access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities makes life simpler.

Our temple-history-themed waiting area showcases the history of the local temple and offers comfortable seating.

Our family research center supports Family Search and ordinance work.

We offer family history consultation and family ordinance card service to make your temple and family history work easier.

Free with Hotel Stay

Enjoy our full breakfast bar with waffles, pastries, toast, yogurt, fruit, cereal, bacon, sausage, eggs, hash browns, juice, milk, and cocoa.

Stay fit with our on-site gym, including ellipticals, treadmills, weight-lifting equipment, and more. Free single-use earbuds keep the noise level peaceful.

Our indoor heated swimming pool and hot tub will help you to refresh and relax after travel, temple service, or anything you're up to.

Wi-Fi with high-speed internet access and smart TVs can help you to stay connected. We know how important staying in touch is!
