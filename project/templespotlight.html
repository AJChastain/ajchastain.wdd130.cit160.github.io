Temple Spotlight: Oakland, California

Historic Details

The Oakland, California Temple was announced on 23 January 1961 and its groundbreaking followed on 26 May 1962. It was originally dedicated on 17 November 1964. After massive renovations, it was rededicated on 16 June 2019.
