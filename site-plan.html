Overview

Purpose

To provide information about services offered by Temple Inn & Suites and promote business, particularly among temple patrons and visitors.

Audience

Members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who visit nearby temples as patrons for ordinances or as visitors for sealings, weddings, youth trips, and related events such as receptions. Needs may include changing rooms, child care, family history center, catering for large events, and long-term stay options for full-time temple missionaries.
