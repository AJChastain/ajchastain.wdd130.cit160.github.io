Chocolate Chip Cookies

These are absolutely da bomb. That is, everyone who likes chocolate chip cookies loves these. Cookies make people happy. You can totally do this.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks (1 cup total) butter or margarine, softened
  • ¾ cup brown sugar, packed in tight. Stuff it in there!
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 ¼ cups flour
  • 1 ½ or 2 cups chocolate chips- any flavor

Instructions

  • Soften the butter by leaving it at room temperature for a couple of hours, or cheat and microwave it for a few seconds. Microwave powers vary, so watch carefully and do not melt it. About 15 seconds should do it.
  • Combine butter and sugars in a large mixing bowl. Cream vigorously by hand or use a mixer until light and fluffy.
  • Add the eggs and vanilla to the sugar and butter. Mix well.
  • Add the salt, baking soda, and some of the flour. Stir.
  • Add the rest of the flour. Stir carefully. (So the flour doesn’t end up all over the kitchen.)
  • Stir in the chocolate chips. Don’t over-stir at this point.
  • Preheat the oven to 375°.
  • Scoop rounded spoonfuls of cookies dough onto a greased cookie sheet.
  • Bake for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.
