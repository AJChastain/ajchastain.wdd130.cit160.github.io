What on Earth is Da Bomb Nom?

Have you ever searched the internet for a recipe, only to be bombarded with lengthy (and utterly unnecessary) blog posts about a food blogger's life history in relation to that food? Da Bomb Nom seeks to eliminate the fluff. Let's get to the point: food. How to follow basic recipes, deliciously.

Why?

You have a life.
You want to cook something.
You can get straight to the recipe without sifting through lengthy fluff.
Clear instructions written by and for real humans makes it easy!

It's Da Bomb!
